Gallery Beyond · Class of 2026

Gallery Beyond is a digital graduation exhibition celebrating the Class of 2026. It transforms student projects into an immersive, explorable space — where design, storytelling, and technology extend beyond the limits of a physical gallery.

Visitors are invited to move through a virtual exhibition hall, discover projects at their own pace, and experience works in a way that feels personal, atmospheric, and interactive — like stepping into a world built around each creator's vision.

This exhibition is both a showcase and a threshold: a moment that marks the end of one chapter, and the start of infinite possibilities beyond graduation.
